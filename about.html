About us

Measurable insight from uncrewed systems

Atlas Geospatial Solutions is a new company built on a simple idea: put the right uncrewed platform and sensor on a mission, capture clean data, and turn it into decisions you can act on. We work with builders, inspectors, filmmakers, and operators who need reliable aerial and geospatial results without the guesswork.

Our approach

Every project starts with the mission, not the hardware. We plan the flight, capture the data, process it with industry-standard photogrammetry and 3D pipelines, and deliver outputs your team can actually use — maps, models, inspections, and footage.

Along the way we help pilots of all levels get airborne safely through FPV and UAV operation instruction.
